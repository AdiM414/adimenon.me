Featured Project: Fireguard

Autonomous Wildfire Response Robot

Fireguard is an engineering project focused on wildfire response and community safety. The goal is to explore how robotics, sensors, and autonomous systems can help detect, navigate, and respond to dangerous wildfire conditions.

This project combines mechanical design, electronics, software, testing, and iteration. It reflects my interest in using engineering to solve real-world problems.
